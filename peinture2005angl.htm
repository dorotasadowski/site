Arbres

For me, Nature is without artifice. I seek to paint the exchange which settles between it and me lasting my walks. I discussions with it a language of silence and glance. My directions seek to feel the rough elements which make it up. By looking at nature, I see who I am. I do not seek to dominate it but to harmonize myself with it through a set of colors. A play where I accompany his daily by living his shades, its light, its seasons, its odors. A play where the brush traces our conversation.
