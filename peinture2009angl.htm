flesh tree

With its roots planted in the earth and its branches aimed to the sky, the tree represents in the same capacity as the man ' « to be both worlds » and the Creation which joins the top and the down. The top, the celestial world represents the domain of mind, the down, the world of the Earth, that of material; ; the man considers himself like a man coming of both worlds, among which he must find his own way. The tree with colour flesh is one of these ways...
